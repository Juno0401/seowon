How to play
• Pick your squad above (up to 5), then deploy with the troop cards (or keys 1–5 for your equipped slots).
• Each troop has a cost and a cooldown ring — cents pour in over time, so spend wisely.
• Upgrade your Worker (Q) for faster income, or Repair (R) your keep.
• The battlefield is wide — scroll with the mouse wheel, by dragging the field, with ←/→, or tap the minimap up top.
• Cards show a D-–A+ scorecard (PWR/HP/SPD/RNG). Take enough hits and a unit gets knocked back — tanks resist it best.
• Stages 1–10 are medieval; from Stage 11 on, the future arrives. Clear stages to unlock troops: Minimen (3) · Gunslinger (4) · UFO (6) · Master Mage (7) · Bombthrower (10) · Cyberwarrior (11) · Necromancer (13). Allies pass through each other; smash the enemy keep before they smash yours.
